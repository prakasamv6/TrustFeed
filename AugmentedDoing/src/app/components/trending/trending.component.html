How Circuit Breakers Work

  • Monitor — Track velocity, AI ratio, coordination, diversity
  • Detect — AI floods, bot campaigns, suspicious spikes
  • Alert — Issue watch/warning/critical alerts
  • Break — Suppress trend, label posts, throttle engagement
  • Review — Human moderators verify and release

Why This Matters

Fake trends manufactured by AI bots and coordinated campaigns can manipulate public opinion and spread misinformation. Circuit breakers are a corrective mechanism to ensure trending topics reflect genuine human interest, not artificial manufacture.
